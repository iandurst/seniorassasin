Standard Rules

  • Play fair and respect others.
  • Follow all local laws and school policies.
  • Admin decisions are final for safety.

Purge Window (Admin‑Triggered)

The Admin may declare a 3‑hour Purge. When a Purge starts:

  • All players are notified by SMS immediately.
  • A countdown timer may be displayed on the site during the Purge.
  • At the end of 3 hours, normal rules automatically resume.

Note: Admin may end the Purge early; if that happens, normal rules resume at once.

Immunity Items

The following items grant temporary immunity only when worn exactly as described:

  1. Goggles — Must be worn properly over the eyes. If pushed up to the forehead/hat or hanging around the neck, they do not count.
  2. Pool Floaties (Arm Floaters) — Two total (one on each arm) and they must be clearly visible outside of whatever outfit the player is wearing.

If either item is removed or not worn as specified, the immunity ends immediately.

Admin Reset (Disclosure)

The Admin Reset is for emergencies only. It clears participants, assignments, votes, and resets settings to defaults.
