Rules & Safety

This game is intended as a safe, water‑tag activity. Always follow local laws and school policies.

  1. Water‑only gameplay: Use water squirters or water balloons. No real or replica weapons, ever.
  2. No school grounds / classes / events: Do not play on campus, during school activities, or where prohibited.
  3. Consent & respect: Do not harass or trespass. Private property requires permission. No play in workplaces without permission.
  4. Safety first: No targeting while driving, biking, or in traffic. No chasing into dangerous places. Be visible at night.
  5. Honor system: Eliminations require clear video/photo proof or neutral confirmation.
  6. Admin decisions are final: Disputes are settled by admins. Sportsmanship is required.
  7. Purge Day: On Saturdays 8:00am–midnight CST, anyone may eliminate anyone. Banner will appear on the site.
  8. Prize Pool: Grows by $5 for each verified elimination.

By participating you agree to these rules. If you see unsafe behavior, report it to admins immediately.
