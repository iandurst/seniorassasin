Official Rules

1) Safety first

  • Use only safe, non-harmful equipment as defined by the organizers.
  • Be respectful and lawful. Trespassing, harassment, or anything unsafe is not allowed.

2) Eligibility & sign up

  • Open to PCS seniors.
  • Sign up with your real first/last name and a working mobile number you can receive texts on.

3) Scoring & eliminations

Eliminations will only be counted if they are recorded and sent to 309‑648‑1257 (Ian Durst’s phone number) or shown to Ian at school.

  • Each verified elimination is worth 1 point to the eliminator.
  • Admins update the leaderboard after verification.

4) Safe zones & no‑go times

  • School, church, and during or directly after sports practices/games are prohibited.
  • Everywhere else is fair game — use common sense and don’t break any laws (especially trespassing).

5) Prize pool & weekly vote

Entry fee is $5 per player, and the pot grows “Squid Game” style: when a player is eliminated, their $5 is added to the prize pool.

  • Every Friday during active weeks there is a vote from 3:00–6:00 PM Central Time.
  • Eligible voters: players still in the game (approved & alive) at the time of the vote.
  • Two choices: Stop the game or Continue.
  • If 75% or more vote to stop, the game ends and the remaining players split the current prize pool. Otherwise, the game continues.
