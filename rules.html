Rules

1) Safety first

  • Only use clearly safe equipment (e.g., foam-dart or water devices if allowed).
  • Never play on campus or during school events.
  • Respect private property and local laws.

2) Eligibility & sign up

  • Open to PCS seniors only.
  • Provide your real name and a reachable phone number for game texts.
  • All sign-ups are reviewed by an administrator before joining the game.

3) Scoring & eliminations

  • Eliminations count only if they are recorded and sent to 309‑648‑1257 (Ian Durst) by text, or shown to him at school.
  • Admins verify eliminations and update the leaderboard.
  • Recording an elimination removes that player from “Alive” and credits 1 elimination to the eliminator.

4) Safe zones & no‑go times

  • School grounds, church, and during or directly after sports practices/games are prohibited.
  • Everywhere else: be smart — do not trespass, don’t break laws, and respect people and property.

5) Prize pool & “Squid Game” style voting

  • Everyone pays a $5 entry fee.
  • The prize pool grows as players are eliminated — each elimination adds $5 to the pot.
  • Every Friday while the game is active, there’s a vote from 3:00–6:00 pm Central.
  • Options: Stop the game or Continue. If 75% of remaining (alive) players vote to stop, the game ends and the remaining players split the prize pool. Otherwise, the game continues.
  • Voting happens on the Voting page using your registered phone number.

6) Conduct

  • Play off school grounds and keep it safe and respectful.
  • Admins may remove any player for unsafe or unsportsmanlike behavior.

How You’ll Receive Your Target

Automated SMS is disabled. After a week is started, the Admin will text each participant their target from 309‑648‑1257 with the message: “your target is <name>”.

Immunity Items

  1. Goggles — Must be worn properly over the eyes. If pushed up or worn on the forehead/hat/neck, they do not count.
  2. Pool Floaties (Arm Floaters) — Two total (one on each arm) and they must be clearly visible outside of any clothing.

Purge Window (Admin‑Triggered)

The Admin may declare a 3‑hour Purge. No automated SMS will be sent; the Admin may notify players manually. A countdown may be shown on the site. When time ends (or the Admin ends it early), normal rules resume immediately.
